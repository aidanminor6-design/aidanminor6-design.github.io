Active Time: 20 minutes, Total Time: 40 minutes 25 Yeild: 16 Cookies
Ingredients
1 cup smooth peanut butter
2/3 cup light brown sugar
1/2 cup plus 1 1/2 tablespoons granulated sugar
1 large egg
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
1 large egg plus 1 egg yolk
Instructions
  1. Heat the oven to 350 degrees F with the oven racks in the upper-middle and lower-middle positions. Line two half sheet pans with parchment paper.
  2. Combine the peanut butter and both sugars in a large bowl. Use a wooden spoon to stir together, then smush the mixture on the sides of the bowl several times to incorporate the sugar.
  3. Add the egg, baking soda, vanilla, and salt. Stir until really smooth.
  4. Use a 1 1/2-tablespoon-sized disher to scoop the dough out into 16 rounds, evenly spacing 8 dough rounds per sheet.
  5. Bake until just set, about 10 minutes, rotating the baking sheets between the racks after about 5 minutes. Let the cookies cool on the sheet pans, set on wire racks, for at least 10 minutes before devouring.
  • use a fork and make hatch marks along the dough to make the cookies cook more evenly
  • use an electric mixer to make mixng easier
